Rooted in Excellence

POWERRANGO was established in 2016 in Gomoa Manso, Central Region, with a bold mission: produce the highest quality palm oil while preserving biodiversity and uplifting local communities.

We believe that the land gives us everything, and we give back through ethical, deforestation-free practices. Our palm oil is crafted with care, from mature plantations to modern processing methods, ensuring pure, nutrient-rich oil.

Today, we are recognized as the best palm nut farm in Gomoa Manso, blending traditional expertise with sustainable innovation. We work hand-in-hand with smallholder farmers, offering fair wages and training programs.

Our Mission

Producing high-quality sustainable palm oil, preserving biodiversity, ensuring fair labor practices, and maintaining deforestation-free supply chains that benefit local communities.

Our Vision

Becoming a leader in sustainable palm nut production, preserving the environment, and empowering local farmers across Ghana and beyond.

Our Premium Offerings

Top-Quality Palm Oil

Cold-pressed, rich in carotene and antioxidants. Our signature palm oil is perfect for culinary, industrial, and cosmetic use — pure and sustainably sourced.

Sustainable Agro-Management

Regenerative agriculture, zero-burning methods, and water conservation. We ensure every hectare supports biodiversity and soil health.

Farmer Empowerment

Training local outgrowers, providing seedlings, and fair trade partnerships to boost rural livelihoods and ethical palm oil production.

Modern Processing Hub

State-of-the-art milling and refining facility ensuring highest hygiene, traceability, and efficiency — from fresh fruit bunches to premium oil.
